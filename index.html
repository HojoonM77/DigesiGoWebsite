How It Works

Three steps to clarity.

DigestiGo turns your natural language into structured health data — no manual entry ever.

01

Chat Naturally

Type what you ate or how you feel — exactly as you'd say it. "I had pizza for lunch and feel bloated" is all it takes. No special format required.

02

AI Categorises & Saves

The AI reads your message, classifies it as a meal, symptom, or food trigger, and stores a timestamped record automatically — while also responding conversationally.

03

Review & Export

Check your Tracker dashboard for a summary of symptoms, triggers, and dietary patterns. When you're ready, generate a PDF report to share with your doctor.
