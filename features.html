Under the Hood

How DigestiGo understands you.

DigestiGo's AI is built on a natural language processing pipeline fine-tuned on thousands of annotated health conversations. It can parse intent, extract entities, infer severity, and generate contextual responses — all in under a second.

Read Our Story →

1. Intent Classification

The AI classifies your message into one or more categories: food, activity, symptom, or energy. A single message can span multiple categories.

2. Entity Extraction

Named entities are extracted — food items, activities, symptom types, durations, quantities, and severity indicators — from unstructured text.

3. Nutritional Inference

For food entries, the engine estimates calories, fiber, macros, and FODMAP category using a nutritional knowledge base mapped to 8,000+ foods.

4. Structured Logging

Extracted data is stored as structured records — typed, timestamped, and tagged — making it queryable for the insights engine.

5. Contextual Response

A response generator produces a clinically-grounded, personalised reply — logging confirmation plus a relevant health tip — in natural language.
