Our Mission

Built for people who
want to understand their health.

DigestiGo started from a simple frustration: managing your health shouldn't require spreadsheets, symptom diaries, or clicking through endless dropdowns. We built a better way.

"Tracking your health should be as simple as having a conversation — not filling out a form."

Millions of people manage chronic conditions, dietary sensitivities, or general health goals with inadequate tools. Most spend months trying to identify their triggers through guesswork and inconsistent manual logging.

Traditional food and symptom diaries require manual data entry that nobody keeps up with. Existing apps treat health tracking like a calorie counter — missing the nuance that actually matters for understanding how you feel day to day.

DigestiGo was designed from the ground up to make health tracking effortless. Our AI understands natural language, extracts structured data from a single sentence, and builds a clear picture of your patterns over time.

Our mission: make meaningful health tracking accessible to everyone, through the most natural interface there is — talking.

Our Story

From a patient's frustration
to a practical tool.

Our co-founder, Alex, was diagnosed with Crohn's disease in 2019. Like many patients, she was handed a paper food diary at discharge and told to "track what triggers flares." Three months of inconsistent logging later, she had data too scattered to be actionable.

She partnered with a gastroenterologist and a machine learning engineer to ask: what if you could just tell an app what you ate, and it figured out the rest?

DigestiGo launched in 2023. Today, it's used by patients managing digestive conditions, general wellness goals, and by clinicians who want better data from their patients between appointments.

Our Values

What we stand for.

Clinically Grounded

Every feature is designed in consultation with clinicians. We don't ship wellness fluff — we build tools that help patients and practitioners have better conversations about health.

Privacy First

Your health data is deeply personal. DigestiGo is built with end-to-end encryption, zero data selling, and full data portability. You own your data — always.

Radically Simple

If it takes more than a sentence to log something, we've failed. DigestiGo's entire interface philosophy is built on the premise that health tracking should feel like texting a friend.

Accessible to All

Health conditions don't discriminate. DigestiGo is committed to accessibility — including screen reader support, plain-language AI responses, and affordable pricing.

Evidence-Based

Our insights and recommendations are backed by peer-reviewed research. We cite our sources and update as science evolves.

Patient-Centred

Everything we build starts with patient feedback. Our advisory board includes people living with IBS, Crohn's, GERD, and other conditions — not just clinicians.
