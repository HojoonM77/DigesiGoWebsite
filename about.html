Our Mission

Built for people who
want to understand their health.

DigestiGo started from a simple frustration: managing your health shouldn't require spreadsheets, symptom diaries, or clicking through endless dropdowns. We built a better way.

"Tracking your health should be as simple as having a conversation — not filling out a form."

Most health tracking apps treat logging like a chore — endless dropdowns, manual entry, calorie counts nobody asked for. We wanted something different.

DigestiGo was built around one idea: the easiest way to capture what you ate, how you felt, or what you did is to just say it. One sentence. Done.

Our AI parses natural language into structured data, spots patterns over time, and surfaces insights without you having to dig through spreadsheets. We think health tracking should feel like texting — not filling out a form.

Our Values

What we stand for.

Radically Simple

If it takes more than a sentence to log something, we've failed. The whole point is that health tracking should feel like texting — not filling out a form.

Privacy First

Your health data is yours. We don't sell it, share it, or use it to target you with ads. Full stop.

Built for Everyone

Whether you're managing a condition or just curious about how food affects your energy — DigestiGo is designed to be useful regardless of where you're starting from.

Honest Insights

We surface patterns in your data — we don't prescribe, diagnose, or pretend to know more than we do. What you see is what your own data actually shows.

Always Improving

We ship fast, listen to feedback, and keep iterating. DigestiGo gets better with every update — and we're just getting started.

User-Driven

Every feature we've built came from a real frustration — our own, or one we heard from people trying to track their health. We build what's actually useful.
